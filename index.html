üìê ESSAY STRUCTURE

Introduction

  1. Start with relevant general statement
  2. Narrow to specific topic (2-3 sentences)
  3. End with thesis statement (your argument)

Body Paragraphs (7-Step Model)

  1. Signpost - What is this about?
  2. State point - Your argument
  3. Support - Expand/explain
  4. Illustrate - Evidence/examples
  5. Infer - What does this mean?
  6. Link - Connect to question
  7. Transition - Lead to next
Conclusion - DO:
‚úì Summarize main argument
‚úì Highlight why it matters
‚úì Link back to question
Conclusion - DON'T:
‚úó Introduce new information
‚úó Just repeat introduction
‚úó Be abrupt ("hit and run")
